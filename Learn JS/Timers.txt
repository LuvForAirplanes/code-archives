Two Types of timers:
one-shot timers
continually firing timers

Oneshot timers:
Declaration-var timerID = setTimeout(yourFunction, millisecondsDelay); - or - timerID(function, milliseconds);
when a timer is declared the second way it can not be accessed by the code
clearTimeout(timerID);



continually Firing timers:
Declaration-var timerID = setInterval(yourFunction, milliseconds); - or - timerID(function, milliseconds);
clearInterval(timerID);
